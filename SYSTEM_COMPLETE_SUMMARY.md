## 🔑 CHED GRADING SYSTEM

### Formula (Official Philippines)

```
KNOWLEDGE (40%):
  - Quizzes Q1-Q5: 40% of knowledge
  - Exams (PR + MD): 60% of knowledge

SKILLS (50%):
  - Output: 40%
  - Class Participation: 30%
  - Activities: 15%
  - Assignments: 15%

ATTITUDE (10%):
  - Behavior: 50%
  - Awareness: 50%

FINAL GRADE = (K × 0.40) + (S × 0.50) + (A × 0.10)
```

### Support for 2 Terms

- **Midterm:** Prelim + Midterm exams
- **Final:** Midterm (carried) + Final exam
- Separate grading forms for each
